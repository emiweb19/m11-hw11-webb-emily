About Me

Hi, I'm Emily Webb, a writer and marketer with an extensive background in higher education communications. I'm passionate about great storytelling and helping organizations meet their strategic goals by crafting compelling narratives and engaging content.

I am a lifelong lover of reading and writing, and I chose to study journalism in my undergrad precisely because with every article, I got to be a storyteller. Through my interviews, I would get insight into a new world, and I would then be responsible for introducing that world to my readers in a way they would comprehend and appreciate.

As I transitioned my career to marketing and public relations, I kept storytelling at the root of all my work, whether I'm writing about a $30 million grant for a campus facilities renovation, sending emails to prospective students, or developing a content plan for a Giving Day campaign.

Career Goals

I currently work as the associate director of marketing and enrollment communications at Lees-McRae College where I have had the opportunity to tell incredible stories about this unique school and its students. I am also pursuing a master's degree in Mass Communications, with a specialization in Public Interest Communications, at the University of Florida. Following graduation, I intend to pursue new leadership opportunities working in nonprofit marketing and communications.

Hobbies

Outside of work, I try to keep myself busy. I enjoy reading, watching movies and TV shows, playing video games, traveling, exploring my town, playing board games, crafting, spending time with my husband and pets, and picking up new hobbies!
